Delete your Together account

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

1. Account Deletion

You can delete your Together account at any time. We respect your data ownership and privacy choices, making it simple to erase your profile and associated data permanently.

2. How to delete your account

Follow these steps within the Together mobile application:

  1. Open Together.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion.

3. What happens when you delete your account

  • Your account is permanently deleted.
  • All personal data is deleted from our servers.
  • Session history, profile information and related data are removed.
  • Backups are automatically removed within 30 days.

4. If you cannot access the app

If you are unable to access the app or sign into your account, you can request account and data deletion by sending an email directly to our support team:
