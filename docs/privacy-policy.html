Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Together. We value the trust you place in us to safeguard your focus and co-working environments. We recognize that virtual body doubling requires sharing focus spaces, and we are committed to ensuring your personal data remains private and protected.

This Privacy Policy explains how Together collects, processes, and stores your information. Our core philosophy is data minimization: we only request permissions necessary to deliver our body doubling matches and virtual companion prompts, and we do not monitor or save your focus interactions.

2. Information We Collect

Together functions as a privacy-respecting application. We collect minimal telemetry to keep the app operational:

  • Display Name & Profile Data: When starting the application, you can create a local profile with a display name (e.g. "John" or "StudyCoder"). This name is visible to peers during matching.
  • Focus Session Telemetry: We log basic, non-identifiable session data (duration, selected task category like "Coding" or "Study", and connection style). This is aggregated to show streaks and progress.
  • Subscription Status: If you upgrade to Together Premium, billing states and subscription entitlements are tracked anonymously using transaction tokens. We do not collect credit cards or bank details.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information exclusively to provide and improve the co-working service:

  • To match you with peer body doubles starting similar focus intervals.
  • To track your local focus streaks and render personal history reports in your app dashboard.
  • To validate billing entitlements for premium focus features.
  • We do NOT sell, license, or trade your data to third parties, and we do not use tracking SDKs for advertisement profiling.

4. Audio & Video Privacy

Together supports voice and camera co-working to replicate real-life visual body-doubling. These connection styles require microphone and camera permissions.

WebRTC Connection & Security: Peer focus sessions utilize the open-source WebRTC protocol to establish a direct, real-time media connection between matching participants. The audio and video channels are protected using **DTLS (Datagram Transport Layer Security)** and **SRTP (Secure Real-time Transport Protocol)**, meaning all streams are encrypted end-to-end in transit between your devices.

Live session ephemerality: Together does not store, record, analyze, or monitor ordinary video feeds or audio logs. When firewalls block direct connection, streams are routed ephemerally through LiveKit servers. LiveKit acts as an in-memory packet router (SFU) and does not inspect the encrypted payload or write media packets to disk.

Report exception: If a camera-session report is submitted, Together attempts to capture a short proof clip from the active session and sends it to Together moderation storage for human review, as described below.

Sessions exist solely in real-time. The moment you press "Leave" or your focus timer finishes, the connection terminates instantly, and no digital trace remains. Camera and microphone access can be muted or completely disabled in-app at any time.

5. Camera Reports & Proof Clips

If one user reports another during a camera session, Together automatically attempts to capture a short 2-second proof video from the active session and upload it to private Together moderation storage for human review. The report includes both users’ account IDs, display names, the session task, planned duration, elapsed time, connection style, session type, report reason, moderation status, and timestamps.

Proof clips and report metadata are used only to review abuse reports, enforce restrictions, protect users, and keep partner sessions safe. They are not used for advertising, public sharing, or unrelated analytics. Confirmed first-time abuse may result in a 30-day restriction from camera and partner services; repeat abuse may result in a permanent restriction.

6. iCloud Sync & Backup

Together does not maintain a custom centralized account database. Instead, we use your device's native local storage and Apple's secure iCloud framework for data synchronization.

If iCloud Sync is enabled, your focus history, custom tasks, streaks, and settings are uploaded directly to your private iCloud container. This data is fully encrypted and remains under your control; Together developers cannot access or read your iCloud files. You can disable iCloud backup at any time from your device's settings.

7. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted infrastructure providers to deliver reliable, on-demand co-working:

  • LiveKit: Used to establish real-time, low-latency audio/video routing between matching peers. Data streams are processed temporarily on LiveKit nodes in memory and are never written to disk.
  • RevenueCat: Handles premium subscription licensing. It matches transaction tokens to unlock stats and virtual companion Liam, without collecting your direct financial details.
