Frequently Asked Questions

Body doubling is a productivity strategy widely utilized by individuals with ADHD, executive dysfunction, or general task-activation barriers. It involves working on tasks (like studying, writing, doing admin, or cleaning) in the presence of another person.

This simple social presence serves as an anchor for the wandering mind, reducing distractibility, increasing motivation, and lowering the neurological "start friction" of tasks without feeling pressuring.

Together features on-demand co-working rooms. Simply choose a task, choose a duration (25, 45, or 60 minutes), and choose a connection preference. The system matches you with a global partner starting a task in less than 10 seconds.

You do not have to speak or show your camera. Together supports three modes:

  • Silent: Perfect for quiet companionship. You work in silence with a simple text indicator that your partner is online and working.
  • Voice: Best for checking in verbally, announcing goals, or receiving quick encouraging words.
  • Camera: Traditional visual body doubling, creating a shared study-room atmosphere.

Liam is our built-in virtual focus companion, ideal for users experiencing social anxiety or who prefer a quiet session without peer matching.

Liam is not an AI: He is a pre-programmed virtual study partner. All check-ins and prompts are processed locally on your device timer. Together does not call external generative AI APIs or transmit your focus data, prompts, or notes to any cloud artificial intelligence services.

Liam is programmed to check in gently every 20 minutes (via quiet voice prompt or card alert, depending on your choice) to ask how your task is progressing, disrupting distraction loops.

Together uses peer-to-peer WebRTC connections routed through secure LiveKit media routers. All live voice and video streams are encrypted in transit using industry-standard **DTLS-SRTP encryption**, protecting you from eavesdropping.

No recording occurs: Audio and video data is streamed in real-time between devices and is never cached, processed, or saved to servers. Metadata (such as focus category and match duration) is stored locally on your device and backed up under your control to iCloud.

Together leverages secure iCloud persistence. When enabled in your Profile, your local focus configurations, completed session history, active streaks, longest streak, and custom tasks are automatically backed up to your personal iCloud container.

This ensures your progress is safely stored and matches up instantly across your iPhone, iPad, and Apple Silicon Mac without requiring you to create an external account.

Together is free to download and use for standard solo sessions and peer focus sessions. We offer a premium subscription ("Together Premium") that unlocks unlimited focus rooms with Liam, detailed focus statistics charts, and custom task icons.

Subscriptions are securely managed through Apple's App Store and Google Play billing, processed via RevenueCat integrations.
