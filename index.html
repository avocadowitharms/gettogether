The Science of
ADHD Body Doubling

Body doubling is an empirically backed focus strategy popular in the neurodivergent community. By working on tasks in the physical or virtual presence of another individual, your brain creates a passive field of social accountability.

For ADHD brains facing executive dysfunction, starting a task represents a massive cognitive hurdle. Having a "body double" working alongside you stabilizes dopamine levels, reduces the feeling of task-related dread, and gently prompts you back to focus if your attention wanders.

Together takes this principle and keeps it practical: start solo, join real body-doubling rooms (Silent, Voice, or Camera), or launch instant 24/7 Focus Spaces to work alongside quiet prerecorded focus environments.

Security & Safety Architecture

Together is built with native device protection, cloud-enforced safety rules, and strict privacy controls to keep your co-working environment safe and focused.

shield

Cloud Database Source of Truth

For signed-in Supabase users, restrictions, blocks, reports, friends, profile history, and visibility settings are cloud-backed with Row-Level Security (RLS). Active restrictions are verified in the cloud before starting partner sessions.

screenshot_monitor

Native Screenshot Protection

Partner sessions activate native screen protection. iOS detects screenshot attempts; Android natively blocks screenshots and listens for Android 14+ capture callbacks. Repeated attempts trigger an automatic 4-hour partner-session restriction.

report

Structured Safety & Evidence Handling

Submit reports with typed categories (Under 18, Harassment, Sexual Content, Threats, Spam/Scam, Safety Concern). Reporting immediately exits the room, restricts the reported user, and uploads encrypted proof clips with zero local file path retention.

block

Explicit Blocking & Exclusions

Explicitly blocking a user creates a cloud DB record. Matching algorithms fetch block records at start to permanently exclude blocked users, keeping blocking and reporting distinct and transparent.

visibility_off

Friend Privacy & Visibility Controls

Friend requests and accepted friends use Supabase RLS policies. Users can disable incoming friend requests entirely and hide their focus streak from public view.

delete_forever

Account Cleanup & Right to Erasure

Camera and voice sessions require explicit device permissions and authenticated identity verification. Account deletion purges local profile data and permanently removes the Supabase auth user along with associated cloud data.
