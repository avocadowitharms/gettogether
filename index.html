What is Together?

Together is an on-demand virtual body doubling and focus companion app for iPhone and Android. It combines 1:1 Live Peer Sessions (Silent, Voice, or Camera), 24/7 prerecorded Focus Spaces, Solo timers, and native screenshot protection in one privacy-first focus app. It is designed especially for people with ADHD and neurodivergents who lose track of time, struggle with executive dysfunction or task initiation, or get pulled away by scheduling friction and camera anxiety. The Science of
ADHD Body Doubling

Body doubling is an empirically backed focus strategy popular in the neurodivergent community. By working on tasks in the physical or virtual presence of another individual, your brain creates a passive field of social accountability.

For ADHD brains facing executive dysfunction, starting a task represents a massive cognitive hurdle. Having a "body double" working alongside you stabilizes dopamine levels, reduces the feeling of task-related dread, and gently prompts you back to focus if your attention wanders.

Together takes this principle and keeps it practical: start solo, join real body-doubling rooms (Silent, Voice, or Camera), or launch instant 24/7 Focus Spaces to work alongside quiet prerecorded focus environments.

Focusmate Comparison

Focusmate pioneered desktop 1:1 webcam body doubling. Sessions require booking fixed time slots in advance (25, 50, or 75 min) and mandate active camera presence. Together eliminates pre-scheduling friction with instant peer matching, provides Silent and Voice options for camera-shy users, and offers 24/7 prerecorded Focus Spaces when no live partner is desired.

FLOWN & Flow Club Comparison

Security & Safety Architecture

Together is built with native device protection, cloud-enforced safety rules, and strict privacy controls to keep your co-working environment safe and focused.

shield

Cloud Database Source of Truth

For signed-in Supabase users, restrictions, blocks, reports, friends, profile history, and visibility settings are cloud-backed with Row-Level Security (RLS). Active restrictions are verified in the cloud before starting partner sessions.

screenshot_monitor

Native Screenshot Protection

Partner sessions activate native screen protection. iOS detects screenshot attempts; Android natively blocks screenshots and listens for Android 14+ capture callbacks. Repeated attempts trigger an automatic 4-hour partner-session restriction.

report

Structured Safety & Evidence Handling

Submit reports with typed categories (Under 18, Harassment, Sexual Content, Threats, Spam/Scam, Safety Concern). Reporting immediately exits the room, restricts the reported user, and uploads encrypted proof clips with zero local file path retention.

block

Explicit Blocking & Exclusions

Explicitly blocking a user creates a cloud DB record. Matching algorithms fetch block records at start to permanently exclude blocked users, keeping blocking and reporting distinct and transparent.

visibility_off

Friend Privacy & Visibility Controls

Friend requests and accepted friends use Supabase RLS policies. Users can disable incoming friend requests entirely and hide their focus streak from public view.

delete_forever

Account Cleanup & Right to Erasure

Camera and voice sessions require explicit device permissions and authenticated identity verification. Account deletion purges local profile data and permanently removes the Supabase auth user along with associated cloud data.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Together, virtual body doubling, and how we compare to other focus services.

How does Together compare to Focusmate for ADHD?

Together is designed specifically around neurodivergent task activation and cognitive barriers. While Focusmate requires scheduling sessions in advance and relies heavily on mandatory webcam presence, Together lets you start immediately with instant 1:1 peer matching, 24/7 prerecorded Focus Spaces (zero wait time), and flexible Silent / Voice connection modes to eliminate camera anxiety. Read our detailed Focusmate Alternative Analysis.

How does Together compare to FLOWN, Flow Club, and Dubbii?

FLOWN and Flow Club host scheduled, facilitator-led video group calls priced between $25 and $40 per month. Together offers private 1:1 sessions and 24/7 ambient Focus Spaces on-demand without monthly subscription barriers, group call pressure, or host introductions. Dubbii offers task videos, whereas Together integrates chore and desk focus with real-time peer body doubling and Pomodoro timers. Explore Together vs. FLOWN and Together vs. Flow Club.

What are 24/7 Focus Spaces?

Focus Spaces are 24/7 instant prerecorded virtual focus environments (quiet office, studying, reading, coding, or household chores) that let you double your focus immediately. They deliver the grounding social facilitation effect of a body double without waiting for a peer match or turning on a camera. Learn more in our Focus Spaces Guide.

How does virtual body doubling relieve executive dysfunction and task paralysis?

Executive dysfunction makes task initiation feel overwhelming due to dopamine gating and decision fatigue. Working alongside a body double activates passive social facilitation, stabilizing focus, grounding attention, and lowering the cognitive activation energy required to start. Read our guide on Body Doubling for Executive Dysfunction.

Is Together privacy-first and secure?

Yes. Together provides free core access and encrypts peer video/audio streams via DTLS-SRTP WebRTC with no server-side video recording or storage. In addition, Together features native iOS screenshot detection, Android screenshot blocking, cloud database Row-Level Security, and explicit block rules.
