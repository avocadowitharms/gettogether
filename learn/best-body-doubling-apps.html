Top Body Doubling Apps Overview:

  • Focusmate: Best for scheduled 1:1 webcam co-working on desktop.
  • Together: Best for privacy-first 1:1 sessions (Silent/Voice/Camera), instant 24/7 Focus Spaces, and mobile focus.
  • Flown: Best for host-guided community drop-in groups ("Flocks").
  • Flow Club: Best for small, member-hosted group co-working with music playlists.
  • dubbii: Best for short prerecorded videos targeting household chore initiation.

Choosing the Right App for Your Needs

When selecting a body doubling platform, consider:

  1. Scheduling vs On-Demand: Do you prefer booking sessions in advance or starting immediately when inspiration hits?
  2. Group vs 1:1: Do small groups or private 1:1 companions help you focus better?
  3. Camera Requirements: Do you want active visual accountability or silent/prerecorded options?
