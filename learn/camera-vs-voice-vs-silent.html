Camera vs Voice vs Silent Sessions: Picking the Right Mode for Your Energy

How to match your body doubling connection style to your daily capacity, task demands, and comfort level.

AT
Written by Ava Thalheim • Updated July 2026
lightbulb Key Takeaways (Direct Answer)

Selecting Your Session Mode in Together:

  • Silent Mode: Best for deep focus, sensitive documents, low social battery, or autistic parallel play.
  • Voice Mode: Best for quick verbal goal setting, momentum check-ins, or audio-first productivity.
  • Camera Mode: Best for traditional visual co-working atmosphere when active visual accountability is desired.
  • Focus Spaces: Best for instant 24/7 visual presence with zero live matching.

Mode Selection Guide

Connection Mode Social Demand Visual Stream Best For
Silent Mode Zero Status Indicator Deep work, introverted focus, sensitive data
Voice Mode Low (Brief verbal check-in) Audio indicator Goal declaration, swift check-ins
Camera Mode Moderate (Shared video) Live P2P Video Traditional body doubling, desk work
Focus Spaces Zero Prerecorded video Instant 24/7 start, zero wait time
