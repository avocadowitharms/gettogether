Together vs Focusmate: Feature Comparison & Differences

A neutral, fact-based breakdown of Focusmate and Together to help you decide which body doubling platform suits your productivity style.

AT
Written by Ava Thalheim • Updated July 2026
lightbulb Key Takeaways (Direct Answer)

Together vs Focusmate Comparison Summary:

  • Focusmate Strengths: Well-established web platform with large active global user base, 25, 50, and 75-minute scheduled sessions, and strong community guidelines.
  • Together Strengths: Offers three distinct session modes (Solo, 1:1 Live Peer, and 24/7 prerecorded Focus Spaces), Silent/Voice/Camera options, and privacy-first peer-to-peer streams.
  • Primary Difference: Focusmate focuses exclusively on scheduled live 1:1 webcam sessions; Together provides flexible on-demand options including non-live 24/7 Focus Spaces for immediate, zero-wait presence.

What Focusmate Does Well

Focusmate pioneered virtual body doubling and remains an excellent platform. Key strengths include:

  • A vast worldwide user base ensuring easy scheduling across all time zones.
  • Structured 25, 50, and 75-minute sessions.
  • Thorough community moderation and rating systems.

Where Together Differ

Together was designed specifically to address social friction, camera exhaustion, and scheduling barriers:

  • 24/7 Focus Spaces: When you need visual company instantly without waiting for a partner match or talking to anyone, Focus Spaces provide 0-wait prerecorded focus environments.
  • Silent Mode: Work alongside a live peer in complete silence with simple active indicators.
